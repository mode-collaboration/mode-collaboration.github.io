        ## 2023

        - **Oct 3:** The preprint *End-To-End Optimization of the Layout of a Gamma Ray Observatory*, led by several MODE members, is now online. [arXiv:2310.01857](https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.01857)
        - **Sep 25:** The preprint *TomOpt: Differential optimisation for task- and constraint-aware design of particle detectors in the context of muon tomography* is now online. [arXiv:2309.14027](https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.14027)
        - **May 30:** Several members of MODE (Aehle, Baydin, Gauger, Haack, Heinrich, Sagebaum, Vischia, Watts) will be present at *Differentiable and Probabilistic Programming for Fundamental Physics*, a long research workshop in Munich. [Participants](https://www.munich-iapbp.de/probabilistic-programming/participants)
        - **May 25:** The MODE white paper *Toward the end-to-end optimization of particle physics instruments with differentiable programming* has been published. [Read](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2405428323000047)
        - **May 18:** The first confirmed contributions to the Third MODE Workshop are published in the agenda: [Indico](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1242538/)
        - **Mar 20:** The Third MODE Workshop on Differentiable Programming for experiment design will take place at Princeton University. Registration and abstract submission are open: [Indico](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1242538/)

        ## 2022

        - **Dec 8:** The contribution by MODE (and collaborators) to the NuPECC Long Range Plan 2024 has been made public: [Link](https://indico.ph.tum.de/event/7050/contributions/6355/)
        - **Nov 25:** A MODE collaboration meeting was held, and seven new members were accepted. [Indico](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1224588/)
        - **Sep 12–16:** The Second MODE Workshop was held in Kolymbari, Crete, with 50 in-person and 36 online participants. [Indico](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1145124/)
        - **May 20:** Registration and abstract submission opened for the Second MODE Workshop. [Indico](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1145124/)
        - **Mar 22:** The MODE white paper *Toward the End-to-End Optimization of Particle Physics Instruments with Differentiable Programming* was uploaded to the arXiv. [arXiv:2203.13818](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.13818)
        - **Mar 10:** The article *neos: End-To-End-Optimised Summary Statistics for High Energy Physics* was uploaded to the arXiv. [arXiv:2203.05570](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.05570)
        - **Mar 6:** The article *Deep Regression of Muon Energy with a K-Nearest Neighbor Algorithm* was uploaded to the arXiv. [arXiv:2203.02841](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.02841)
        - **Feb 28:** The article *Differentiable Matrix Elements with MadJax* was uploaded to the arXiv. [arXiv:2203.00057](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.00057)
        - **Feb 22:** The article *Calorimetric Measurement of Multi-TeV Muons via Deep Regression* was published in EPJ C. [Read](https://epjc.epj.org/articles/epjc/abs/2022/01/10052_2022_Article_9993/10052_2022_Article_9993.html)

        ## 2021

        - **Oct 21:** Several new members joined MODE: Dr. Tasos Belias (GSI), Dr. Claudius Krause (Rutgers), Prof. Christian Glaser (Uppsala), Mr. Max Lamparth (TUM), Dr. Lukas Heinrich (CERN), Dr. Patrick Stowell (Durham), Prof. Haitham Zaraket (Lebanese University).
        - **Sep 9:** The first MODE workshop on automatic differentiation was a success, with over 100 participants.
        - **Jul 22:** External registrations opened for the first MODE workshop on automatic differentiation.
        - **May 14:** An organizational meeting of INFN researchers interested in MODE was held, leading to the creation of the MODE_INFN group.
        - **Apr 25:** ParticleBites published a post on MODE. [Read](https://www.particlebites.com/?p=9042)
        - **Apr 21:** The APPEC website and newsletter featured an interview with the Scientific Coordinator of MODE. [Read](https://www.appec.org/news/machine-learning-optimized-design-of-experiments)
        - **Mar 30:** The first MODE article was published in *Nuclear Physics News International*. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1080/10619127.2021.1881364)
        - **Mar 10:** The preprint of the MODE short article became available on INSPIRE. [INSPIRE](http://inspirehep.net/record/1850892)
        - **Mar 1:** The webpage for the first MODE Workshop was made public.
        - **Feb 5:** The preprint of the MODE short article was made public. [PDF](https://mode-collaboration.github.io/docs/mode_npni_preprint.pdf)
        - **Feb 5:** Contact information for the Steering Board and institutional representatives was published.
