The Fifth MODE Workshop on Differentiable Programming for experiment design took place at OAC (Kolymbari, Crete).

Registration and abstract submission were open on the [Indico page](https://indico.cern.ch/event/1481852/).

Keynote talks by Danilo Rezende (DeepMind), Andrea Walther (HU Berlin), and Riccardo Zecchina (Bocconi).
